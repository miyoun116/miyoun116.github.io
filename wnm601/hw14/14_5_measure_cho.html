How to Measure

Please carefully follow our "How to Measure" guide. (UNIT: INCH)

Height

1. Stand at attention.
2. Start from Bulging bone dehind neck.
3. Keep measure until it reaches to the floor and add height of your shoes.
4. Measuring Height is finished.

Arm and Sleeve Length

1. Start from Bulging bone behind neck.
2. Pass by shoulders Point.
3. To back of hand. Keep measure until it reaches to 1 inch below wristbone.
4. Measuring Arm and Sleeve Length is finished.

Neck Width

1. Point where neck and shoulder line meets.
2. Go around neck.

Upperchest

1. Raise both Arm.
2. Go around Chest right below armpit.Don't tighten tapeline too hard.
