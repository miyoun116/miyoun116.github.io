How to Measure

Chest

1. Raise both Arm.
2. Go around Chest right below armpit. Don't tighten tapeline too hard.

Height

1. Start from Bulging bone behind neck.
2. Keep measure until it reaches to the floor and add height of your shoes.

Arm and Sleeve Length / Hwajang

1. Start from Bulging bone behind neck.
2. Pass by shoulders Point.
3. To back of hand. Keep measure until it reaches to 1 inch below wristbone.
