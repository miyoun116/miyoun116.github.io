Korean Traditional Foods

Bulgogi

Bulgogi is a Korean-style grilled or roasted dish. It made of thin, marinated slices of beef or pork grilled on a barbecue or on a stove-top griddle. It is also often stir-fried in a pan in home cooking. Sirloin, rib eye or brisket are frequently used cuts of beef for the dish.

Japchae

Japchae is a sweet and savory dish of stir-fried glass noodles and vegetables that is popular in Korean cuisine. The noodles made from sweet potato starch are topped with assorted vegetables, meat, and mushrooms, and seasoned with soy sauce and sesame oil.

Bibimbap

Bibimbap literally means "mixed rice". Bibimbap is served as a bowl of warm rice topped with vegetables and gochujang (chili pepper paste). A raw or fried egg and sliced meat (usually beef) are common additions. The hot dish is stirred together thoroughly just before eating.
