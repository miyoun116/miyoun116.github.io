abc News Bumper

3D Animated News Bumper / 2019 Fall

Overview

This is a 3D animated news bumper for a school project in a motion graphics course. I created the news bumper using abc News logo and a clock concept that has a variety of camera angle and spinning options.

My Role

• Created 3D objects using imported Illustrator artwork.

• Created advanced camera animations with Cinema 4D and After Effects.

concept

This news bumper features the iconic ABC News logo atop a rotating red hemisphere with intricate clock elements surrounding it. Logo animations are an effective tool to enhance a brand's personality and leave a lasting impact. In this animation, the spinning hemisphere, circles, and diverse camera angles lend the motion a dynamic and energetic feel. The rotating clock in the animation represents the essence of a live broadcast which is swift and accurate.
Furthermore, the color combination of red, white, and gray is one of the most visually striking color schemes, conveying ideas of professionalism, vitality, and passion. Together, these elements create a dynamic and memorable intro that captures the essence of the ABC News brand.
