abc News
Overview

This is a 3D animated news bumper for a school project in a motion graphics course. I created the news bumper using abc News logo and a clock concept that has a variety of camera angle and spinning options.


Timeline

2019 Fall


Project info

A class project


My role

Graphic & UI design


Tools

After Effects, Cinema 4D, Illustrator

Concept

Logo animations are an effective tool to enhance a brand's personality and leave a lasting impact. This news bumper features the iconic ABC News logo atop a rotating red hemisphere with intricate clock elements surrounding it. In this animation, the spinning hemisphere, circles, and diverse camera angles lend the motion a dynamic and energetic feel. The rotating clock in the animation represents the essence of a live broadcast which is swift and accurate.

Color palette

The color combination of red, white, and gray is one of the most visually striking color schemes, conveying ideas of professionalism, vitality, and passion.
