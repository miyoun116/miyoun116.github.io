Charm Credit Card
Overview

This is a hybrid (2D & 3D) animation concept video for a school project in a motion graphics course. It presents information about the benefits of the Charm Credit Card, whose name is derived from the initials of its features: cinema, hotel, airline, restaurant, and market. The animation features match cuts that smoothly transition from one shot to another, linking small stories together.
