Charm Credit Card

Animated Concept Video / 2019 Fall

Overview

This is a hybrid (2D & 3D) animation concept video for a school project in a motion graphics course. It presents information about the benefits of the Charm Credit Card, whose name is derived from the initials of its features: cinema, hotel, airline, restaurant, and market. The animation features match cuts that smoothly transition from one shot to another, linking small stories together.

My Role

Created concept of product, script, and storyboards, and then created an animation using Abode Illustration, After Effects, and Cinema 4D.

Script

Do you enjoy travel, resort activities, dining out, movies, and going to the market? Then more of the things you love while saving your hard-earned money with all the benefits of the CHARM credit card. The CHARM credit card offers a 100 dollar credit for airline fees and an additional 100 dollars for hotel fees every year. Enjoy dining, spa, and resort activities and create memories of a lifetime with your family and friends at qualifying locations. Earn triple membership points and rewards by eating delicious food at restaurants worldwide. And why not follow up dinner with a movie? You can receive 20 percent off movie tickets at any cinema. You can even collect points while running your daily errands. With your CHARM credit card, you can buy your groceries and earn triple membership rewards point at your local supermarket. Make these holidays great for you and your family, with the CHARM credit card.
