Sarrland Museum

Graphic Design / 2019 Spring

Overview

I chose the Saarland Museum for a museum brand course project. The Saarland Museum is a famous museum in southwestern Germany famous for both its old collections and its exhibits of modern art.

My Role

Designed a museum logo, a exhibition poster, a museum website, and a process book for a course project.

Logo Design

I created the museum logo simple and modern using geometric shapes and lines which is inspired of the structure of the museum. The logo is made by 's' and 'm' which is the abbreviation of the Saarland Museum. The image is not only the abbreviation but also a symbolization of the sun and the museum.

Color Palette

The color palette consists of bright, bold, and strong colors. The red, yellow, and black colors represent Saarland in Germany and the gray color is from the museum building.
