Saarland Museum
Overview

For my museum branding project in my graphic design class, I selected the Saarland Museum. The Saarland Museum is a famous museum in southwestern Germany famous for both its old collections and its exhibits of modern art.


Timeline

2019 Spring


Project info

A class project


My role

Graphic design, Process book with a logo, an exhibition poster, and a website


Tools

Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

Logo Design

I created the museum logo simple and modern using geometric shapes and lines which is inspired of the structure of the museum. The logo is made by 's' and 'm' which is the abbreviation of the Saarland Museum. The image is not only the abbreviation but also a symbolization of the sun and the museum.
