Goals and Missions

Intoit is a San Francisco-based start-up that aims to promote natural and holistic approaches to achieving optimal health and well-being. They have undertaken a project to develope a comprehensive branding concept and create an effective UI/UX design for their website launch.

Visual Brand Identity

7 Flower essences, one of the main products of Intoit, contain the vibrational energy signature of 7 chakras. We thought that the story of 7 chakras can engages the users in the process of enjoying and purchasing.

Moodboard

I was inspired from images that possess a minimalist aesthetic, featuring clean lines and bold, vibrant colors that create a strong visual impact. Also, using the rotating motion with transparent overlays, I added dynamics to the identity.
