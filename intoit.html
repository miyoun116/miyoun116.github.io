Intoit

UI and Graphic Design / May, 2022 - Present

Overview

Intoit is a start-up company based in San Francisco. They are preparing for the launch of their plant-based products. Their flower essences, creams and spices have been crafted to deliver the positive, balancing and nourishing energies of each of the 7 Chakras. The project goal is creating UI design for their e-commerce website and developing marketing materials such as business cards, journals, and stickers based on the conceptual framework of the 7 Chakras.

My Role

Lead for the development of UI design and graphic design for animation, infographics, and marketing materials.

Target Audience

Women aged 25-65 years in the Bay Area.

What is the 7 Chakras?

In Hinduism and Buddhism, chakras are focal points of energy in the body. The seven chakras mark places where spiritual energies intersect and are believed to be connected via energy channels. The concept of chakras has influenced many holistic medical practices worldwide, including yoga, Ayurveda, and some modern therapies, such as sound baths. Each chakra relates to specific spiritual, emotional, psychological, and physical aspects.

Design Concept

Each of the seven chakras has a unique symbol with mandalas and colors. These circular symbols use curly and sharp lines to look symmetrical and balanced; they are commonly used for chakras. However, this design does not work well with the INTOIT.LOVE products which have a simple and modern style. Through a target audience interview, I learned that target audiences prefer a simple and modern design rather than a traditional one for skincare products. For this reason, I decided to create a simple and clean icons and user interfaces using geometric shapes to engage with the modern style of the products and to distinguish its simple design from competitors.
