Background

Intoit is a startup company that needs to create a strong brand identity and promote its story, products and services to potential customers. We aim to promote Intoit’s brand identity and products to potential customers through distinctive marketing materials and a website.

UI/UX design
Project goals
  • Create a user-friendly website that ensures easy navigation and delivers a seamless shopping experience for customers.

  • Present Intoit's products in a visually appealing and modern manner, capturing the attention of visitors and compelling them to explore further.

  • Expand Intoit's customer base by reaching a diverse range of individuals interested in health and well-being.

  • Drive online sales and increase overall revenue for the company.
Target audience

All age groups who are enthusiastic about their health and well-being, seeking natural and plant-based solutions.

Problem statement

Intoit faces the challenge of establishing its brand and product offerings in the market due to its relatively new presence. To overcome this, the company recognizes the need to create an informative and engaging website that effectively communicates its unique identity, strengths, and product range. By doing so, Intoit aims to unlock its growth potential, expand its customer base, and establish a strong foothold in the industry.
