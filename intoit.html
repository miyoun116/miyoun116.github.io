Intoit

Summary

Intoit is a start-up company based in San Francisco. They are preparing for the launch of their plant-based products. Their flower essences, lip-balms and a throat spray have been crafted to deliver the positive, balancing and nourishing energies of each of the 7 Chakras. The project goal is creating UI/UX design for their e-commerce website and developing marketing materials such as business cards, stickers and animation based on the conceptual framework of the 7 Chakras.

Overview

Ideation

Each of the seven chakras has a unique symbol with mandalas and colors. These circular symbols use curly and sharp lines to look symmetrical and balanced; they are commonly used for chakras. However, this design does not work well with the Intoit products which have a simple and modern style. Through a target audience interview, I learned that target audiences prefer a simple and modern design rather than a traditional one for skincare products. For this reason, I decided to create a simple and clean icons and user interfaces using geometric shapes to engage with the modern style of the products and to distinguish its simple design from competitors.

Design Process

Inspirations

I was inspired from images that possess a minimalist aesthetic, featuring clean lines and bold, vibrant colors that create a strong visual impact.

Images for Flower Essences

1. Seven Chakras

Intoit specializes in flower essences, which capture the unique vibrational energy of each plant. As part of Intoit's marketing strategy, I have created Chakra images representing the 7 flower essences. These images will be featured on our website and in marketing animations to showcase the essence of each product.

2. Beyond the Five Elements

The first five chakras are associated with the physical elements earth, water, fire(solar), air, and space. The last two chakras are thought to connect us beyond the earthly realm, so they are associated with the elements of light and cosmic energy (Source:https://www.yogajournal.com/practice/yoga-sequences-level/beginners-guide-chakras/).
I have created seven icons representing the elements, using a black and white color scheme that matches the style of our product labels. These icons will be featured on both the product labels and the Intoit website, with the goal of helping users better understand and interpret information.
