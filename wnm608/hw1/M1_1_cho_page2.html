Maui in Hawaii

Maui Tropical Plantation

Maui Tropical Plantation offer the Tropical Express Tour and show Hawaii’s famous fruits and plants. We taste the fresh fruit, ice cream and coffee. This is a nice place to take a picture.

Maui Chef's Table

MAUI’s Chef’s Table is very interesting restaurant. We could see the mountains and the lake out of the window. There were a lot of unique decorations such as a steam train. Food was also good.

LUAU Show

The Hawaii Luau Show is an outstanding show that brings us a unique Polynesian cultural experience through live traditional chants, music, drumming and dancing. They offered Hawaiian Buffet during the show. Traditional dance was beautiful and impressive.
