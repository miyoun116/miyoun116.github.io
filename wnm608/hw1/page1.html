Seoul in Korea

Lotte World Tower

Lotte World Tower is a 123-floor skyscraper. It contains retail outlets, restaurants, offices, residences and a luxury hotel. Seoul Sky is located on the 117th-123rd floors of Lotte World Tower.People can see the scenery of Seoul city with a glass bottom and a telescope.

Pororo Park

Pororo Park is an edutainment space which creates education effect while playing and the place of playing culture to connect kids with friendly Pororo character. Kang visited pororo town where only saw on TV. He was really enjoy this time.

LINA's Paris Restaurant

LINA's Paris is a restaurant that offers sandwiches and dessert. Shaved Ice is one of the favorite dessert of my family. We ate Red Been And Grain Powder Shaved Ice. The weather was hot and shaved ice was cool and tasty.
