A bit more about me
"With a curiosity for user motivation and behavior, I discover meaningful insights and compelling solutions to create user-friendly products that delight and inspire."

I recently graduated from the Academy of Art University in Interaction and UI/UX design and joined Intoit as a UI/ UX designer.


I am a highly motivated and enthusiastic UX designer with a strong passion for Illustration and design systems. I enjoy graphic and UX design work with experience in web and mobile applications. I have experience generating ideas and solutions, conducting user research, and establishing high-fidelity mockups with interactive prototypes.


Before becoming a product designer, I had five years of experience in the healthcare industry as a registered nurse in Korea. This experience has provided me with strong empathetic abilities and a deep understanding of how to effectively communicate and collaborate with diverse groups of people.


I have lived in four different countries: Korea, the US, the UK, and Germany. These experiences have given me a unique perspective on culture and the importance of cross-cultural design.


With a growth mindset, I consistently pursue opportunities to broaden my knowledge and enhance my skills as a designer.
