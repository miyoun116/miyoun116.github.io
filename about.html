A bit more about me
"With a curiosity for user motivation and behavior, I discover meaningful insights and compelling solutions to create user-friendly products that delight and inspire."

I have a strong background in graphic and UX design, with experience in web and mobile application. My UX Design/Research Internship at KAIST gave me hands-on experience in conducting user research, identifying user needs, and creating user-centered apps. I have also honed my graphic and UX design skills as a UX Design Intern at Intoit, where I worked on the company's website, brand imagery, and animations.


Before becoming a product designer, I had five years of experience in the healthcare industry as a registered nurse in Korea. This experience has provided me with strong empathetic abilities and a deep understanding of how to effectively communicate and collaborate with diverse groups of people.


I am excited to use my skills and experience to create user-centered experiences that make a positive impact on the world.
